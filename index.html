Privacy Policy

Thank you for using our app. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is secure. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your personal information when you use our app.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We collect personal information from you when you register for our app, post a product for sale, or contact other users. The personal information we collect includes your name, email address, phone number, and location. We may also collect other information that you voluntarily provide to us when using our app.

2. Use of Personal Information

We use your personal information to provide our services to you, to communicate with you about your account and our app, to respond to your inquiries, and to provide you with marketing materials about our app. We may also use your personal information to improve our app and to develop new features and services.

3. Disclosure of Personal Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we may disclose your personal information to third parties in the following circumstances: With your consent To our service providers who assist us in providing our app to you To comply with applicable laws and regulations To protect our rights or property or the rights or property of our users

4. Security of Personal Information

We take the security of your personal information seriously and use industry-standard security measures to protect it. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Location Information

Our app may collect your location information when you post a product for sale. This information is used to show your product's location to potential buyers. We do not store your location information after the sale has been completed.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may change this privacy policy from time to time by posting an updated version on our app. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at liyumarket.com.
